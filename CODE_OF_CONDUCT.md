# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Python Under The Hood a positive experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are encouraged to:

* Be respectful and constructive.
* Help others learn and grow.
* Give feedback professionally.
* Focus on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

* Harassment or personal attacks.
* Discrimination of any kind.
* Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
* Spam or malicious contributions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Questions

If you have concerns regarding community behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainers.

Let's work together to build a welcoming learning environment for everyone.
